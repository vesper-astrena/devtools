What It Does

Prediction markets are information-rich but time-consuming to monitor. Polymarket Scanner automates the tedious parts.

Arbitrage Detection

Finds contracts where YES + NO prices sum to less than $1.00, or multi-outcome markets with guaranteed profit opportunities.

Edge Scoring

Compares market prices against aggregated polling data, model predictions, and historical base rates to surface mispriced contracts.

Odds Movement Tracking

Monitors price changes over time. Flags sharp moves that may indicate new information or manipulation.

Volume Analysis

Tracks liquidity depth and trading volume. Helps you size positions appropriately and avoid thin markets.

Custom Watchlists

Define the markets you care about. Get alerts only for opportunities that match your criteria.

Export & Alerts

Output to CSV, JSON, or webhook. Pipe to Slack, Discord, or email for real-time notifications.
